About Me

Hi. I'm Nathan. I have been working in the Information Technology industry for the past 14 years. I have an Associate of Science degree in Computer Studies and a Bachelor of Science degree in Cybersecurity. 💻🔒

I am the Founder & Chief Podcaster of Nathan Markley Studios, a media company.

I have been podcasting since 2013 where I started The Nathan Markley Show, where I talk and interview with some of Rhode Island’s best! From entrepreneurs, to content creators to pioneers of the Rhode Island region. 🎙️🎧

When I am not behind the microphone, you'll find me behind the camera, capturing breathtaking moments as a passionate photographer. Nature and Landscapes — “I freeze emotions in time, letting the images speak for themselves.” I have been taking photos since 2006 where I joined a high school photography club.

Beyond my professional endeavors, I proudly hold an Amateur (Ham) Radio License and achieved the rank of Eagle Scout. 📡🦅

With a heart dedicated to leadership and community, I proudly serve on the Board of Directors for the Rhode Island Cross-Disability Coalition. 🤝🌍

I strive to be an influential figure and a source of inspiration for individuals living with disabilities. 🌟💻 My mission is to reach out to as many people as I can and let them know that they can be whomever they want to be and achieve great accomplishments no matter who they are or whatever limiting factors they feel they have. I am living proof of this.
